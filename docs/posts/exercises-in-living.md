# exercises in living

*05 May, 2026*

over the weekend, i had the chance to reread [%%green%%excercises in style%%](https://pzwiki.wdka.nl/mw-mediadesign/images/a/a2/Raymond_Queneau_Exercises_in_Style.pdf) by [%%purple%%raymond queneau%%](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Raymond_Queneau). excellent read, highly recommended.

the book is simple. it takes a small slice of a day and tells it in 99 different ways. the themes are %%yellow%%repitition%%, %%orange%%mundanity%%, and %%cyan%%everyday life%%. it is about how we experience the world around us in so many different ways. %%yellow%%we can all experience the same thing, but walk away with our own story/interpretation of it%%.

it made me think about chores and routine less as a sisyphean struggle and more as an infinite number of chances to tell your story through what you do and how you do it. it is a highly humorous and %%green%%optimistic%% book. %%green%%everyday is an exercise in living%%.
