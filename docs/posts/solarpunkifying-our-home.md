# solarpunkifying our home

*04 Apr, 2026*

## the office, solar powered

🌞 my setup is **dead simple**: i have a vtoman flashspeed 1500 (1548wh, lifepo4) feeding an hp t740 thin client (arch+local llms), two monitors, a macbook air, and a starlink router. a vtoman vs220 Pro portable solar panel setup providing enough power for my nine-hour workdays, partial grid dependence if we have more than 2 consecutively cloudy days which is very rare here.

## the garden

🌧️ a 1100 litre rain catcher, plus a 160 litre rain barrel, and a grey water ground filter into a 5 meter well.

🗑️ 162 litre rotating composter, paper recycling tub (for paper making).

👨🏽‍🌾 planted a 2 x 1 meter milpa bed 🍅🫛🌽🫘, three herb garden tubs 🥬, seeding chilies 🌶️, and planted lime, orange, and mango trees.
